Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Google AdSense eligibility and approval

What is a Google AdSense Approval Checker? +
A free tool that analyzes your website against AdSense eligibility requirements — content quality, privacy policy, site structure, mobile-friendliness — giving you a readiness score and actionable fix list.
How long does AdSense approval take? +
Typically 1–14 business days. Sites that fully meet all requirements tend to get approved faster, sometimes within 24–48 hours.
Why does Google keep rejecting my AdSense application? +
Most common reasons: insufficient original content, missing privacy policy, site too new (under 6 months), free subdomain, prohibited content, or poor user experience. Our checker identifies exactly which apply to your site.
Is this tool free to use? +
Yes — 100% free, forever. No signup required. Enter your URL and get a full eligibility report instantly.
How many articles do I need for AdSense approval? +
While Google doesn't specify an exact number, most approved sites have 20–30 high-quality original articles of 800–1500+ words each. Quality matters far more than quantity.
